Cedarburg Chamber of Commerce: Membership Application

Applicant Information
Membership Level

Free Membership

Cost: Free

  • Access to Chamber of Commerce events and networking opportunities.
  • Listing in the Chamber's online directory.
  • Stay informed with monthly newsletters.
  • Access to educational resources and workshops.

Bronze Membership

Cost: $250 per year

  • All Free Membership benefits, plus:
  • Discounted rates on Chamber-sponsored events and workshops.
  • One featured spotlight position on the Chamber's website per year.
  • Access to Chamber's social media promotion for events or initiatives.
  • Quarterly newsletter feature highlighting Bronze Members.

Silver Membership

Cost: $500 per year

  • All Bronze Membership benefits, plus:
  • Priority placement in the Chamber's online directory.
  • Two featured spotlight positions on the Chamber's website per year.
  • Complimentary admission to quarterly networking events.
  • Additional discounts on advertising opportunities through the Chamber.

Gold Membership

Cost: $1,000 per year

  • All Silver Membership benefits, plus:
  • Highest priority placement in the Chamber's online directory.
  • Four featured spotlight positions on the Chamber's website per year.
  • Exclusive invitation to VIP events and receptions hosted by the Chamber.
  • Priority consideration for speaking opportunities at Chamber Events.
