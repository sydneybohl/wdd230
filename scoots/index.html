Welcome to Scoots Rentals!

Scoots Rentals is the best motor scooter rental company located on the island of Cozumel. We are a owner-operated company that caters to locals, walk-in tourists, motor scooter enthusiasts, and cruise-based touring groups and partnerships. We have two rental locations on Cozumel. In el Centro de Cozumel, we are located by the Playa del Carmen-Cozumel Ferry dock. Our second location is located along the coast, near the Terminal Puerta Maya, for all our visitors that come by cruise.

Here at Scoots Rentals, your premier destination for all your rental needs in Cozumel, Mexico, explore our wide range of vehicles designed to make your island adventure unforgettable.

1. Honda Metropolitan 49cc Scooter: Perfect for zipping around the island with ease, our Honda Metropolitan scooters offer convenience and style. Ideal for solo explorers or couples looking for a fun and efficient way to get around.

2. Honda Dio 110cc Scooter: Step up your scooter game with our Honda Dio models. With a bit more power, these scooters are great for longer excursions or for those who prefer a bit more speed and agility.

3. Honda PCX150 149cc Scooter: Experience ultimate freedom with our Honda PCX150 scooters. Whether you're cruising along the coastline or venturing into the heart of the island, these scooters offer a smooth and reliable ride for all your adventures.

4. Honda Pioneer 1000 ATV: For off-road enthusiasts, our Honda Pioneer 1000 ATV is the perfect choice. Conquer rugged terrain and explore Cozumel's hidden gems with ease and confidence.

5. Jeep Wrangler 4 Door: Take your island exploration to the next level with our spacious Jeep Wrangler 4 Door. Whether you're traveling with family or friends, these rugged vehicles offer comfort and versatility for any adventure.

6. Jeep Wrangler Open-Air 2 Door: Feel the wind in your hair as you cruise around Cozumel in our Jeep Wrangler Open-Air 2 Door models. Perfect for couples or solo travelers seeking the thrill of an open-air driving experience.

At Cozumel Rentals, we pride ourselves on providing top-quality vehicles and exceptional service to ensure your vacation is everything you dreamed of and more. Book your rental today and start exploring all that Cozumel has to offer!
