Cedarburg Chamber of Commerce

Cedarburg Chamber of Commerce. Cedarburg being the city the Chamber of Commerce operates in.

The Cedarburg Chamber of Commerce’s goal is to make Cedarburg the best place to do business and prosper. As an organization, we support our community by creating opportunities to grow and network, promote local business and our community through marketing, and preserve and promote the rich history Cedarburg has to offer.

What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?

We hold a “Morning Meet-Up” at a local coffee shop on the first Friday of every month. In February, we are excited to hold a “Business After Hours” event on February 15th at the Cedarburg Community Center. All additional information can be located under the “Discover” page.

What events are being held in the community this month?

A new pizza shop is opening on February 10th called “Just a Slice.” South Cedarburg School is having a bake sale for its little league team’s new season.

Where can I find the list of businesses that belong to the chamber?

You can find that information under the “Directory” page.

Fonts

My fonts will be 'Raleway' and 'Inter'. Raleway will be used for the headings and Inter will be used for the main writing.
